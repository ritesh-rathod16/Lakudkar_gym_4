7-Day Diet Plan

📊 Daily Macros (Indian Foods)

💪 Protein: 1.6-2.2g/kg (Eggs 🥚, Chicken 🍗, Paneer 🧀, Dal 🌱)

⚡ Carbs: 3-5g/kg (Brown Rice 🍚, Roti 🫓, Oats 🥣)

🫒 Fats: 0.8-1g/kg (Ghee 🧈, Nuts 🥜, Coconut 🥥)

⏰ Meal Timing (When to Eat?)

Pre-Workout (1-2h before) ⚡ – Carbs + protein (banana + peanut butter)

Post-Workout (30-60min after) 💪 – Protein + carbs (chicken + rice)

Snacks 🥜 – Nuts, Greek yogurt, protein shake

🚫 Foods to Limit

Processed Sugar 🍩 – Spikes insulin

Trans Fats 🍟 – Bad for heart

Alcohol 🍺 – Slows recovery

Excess Salt 🧂 – Bloating & BP issues

✅ Healthy Swaps

White rice → Brown rice 🌾

Soda → Sparkling water 🥤➡️💧

Fried chicken → Grilled chicken 🍗🔥

Candy → Dark chocolate (70%+) 🍫

🥗 Micronutrients (Vitamins & Minerals)

Vitamin C 🍊 – Immunity & skin

Vitamin D ☀️ – Bone health

Iron 🥩 – Oxygen transport (spinach, red meat)

Calcium 🥛 – Strong bones

Magnesium 🌰 – Muscle relaxation

💡 Pro Tips

✔ 80/20 Rule – 80% healthy, 20% flexible

✔ Eat the Rainbow 🌈 – More colorful veggies = more nutrients

✔ Listen to Your Body 🧠 – Hunger ≠ cravings

💧 Hydration: 3-4L water + Nimbu Pani 🍋 or Coconut Water 🥥

6-Day Gym Workout Split

🏋️‍♂️ 6-Day Gym Workout Split

Goal: Build muscle 💪, strength 🔥 & endurance 🏃‍♂️

Rest Day: Sunday 🧘 (active recovery: yoga/walking)

🏋️‍♂️ Gym Essentials

Dumbbells 🏋️‍♂️💪 – Versatile for strength training.

Barbell �♂️🔥 – For squats, deadlifts, and bench press.

Treadmill 🏃‍♂️🌀 – Cardio king.

Resistance Bands 🎗️💥 – Portable strength & mobility.

Gym Mat 🧘‍♂️🟢 – Stretching & core workouts.

💪 Popular Workouts

Chest Day 🏋️‍♂️❤️ – Bench press, flys, push-ups.

Leg Day 🦵🔥 – Squats, lunges, deadlifts.

Arm Day 💪🎯 – Bicep curls, tricep dips.

HIIT ⚡⏱️ – High-intensity interval training.

Yoga 🧘‍♀️☮️ – Flexibility & mindfulness.

🍏 Gym Nutrition

Pre-Workout ⚡🥤 – Energy boost.

Protein Shake 🥛💪 – Muscle recovery.

Healthy Meal Prep 🥗🍗 – Chicken, rice, veggies.

Hydration 💧🚰 – Water is a must!

🏆 Gym Culture

Gym Bros 💪🤝 – Spotting & motivation.

Gym Selfies 🤳🔥 – Progress pics.

Gym Fail Memes 😂🚷 – Dropping weights, awkward faces.

Wipe Equipment 🧼🧴 – Hygiene first.

Don't Hog Machines ⏳🙅‍♂️ – Share the gains.

Headphones Rule 🎧🔇 – No loud grunting.

💡 Gym Motivation

"No Pain, No Gain" 💥🏆

"Sweat Now, Shine Later" ✨💦

"Beast Mode ON" 🦍🔥

Detailed Workout Plan

Day 1️⃣ (Monday): Chest & Triceps

Flat Bench Press – 4x6-8 🏋️‍♂️

Incline Dumbbell Press – 3x8-10 🏋️‍♀️

Cable Flys – 3x12 ✨

Triceps Dips – 3xMAX 💪

Skull Crushers – 3x10-12 🦾

(Finish: 10 min incline treadmill walk 🚶‍♂️)

Day 2️⃣ (Tuesday): Back & Biceps

Pull-Ups – 4x6-8 (add weight if advanced) �♂️

Barbell Rows – 3x8-10 🏋️‍♀️

Lat Pulldown – 3x10-12 🔄

Deadlifts – 3x5 (heavy!) ⚡

Hammer Curls – 3x12 💥

(Stretch: 5 min lats/biceps 🧘)

Day 3️⃣ (Wednesday): Legs (Quads Focus)

Squats – 4x6-8 🦵

Bulgarian Split Squats – 3x8/leg 🏋️‍♀️

Leg Press – 3x10-12 🚂

Leg Extensions – 3x15 (slow!) 🐢

Calf Raises – 4x15 👟

(Cool down: 5 min bike 🚴‍♂️)

Day 4️⃣ (Thursday): Shoulders & Abs

Overhead Press – 4x6-8 🏋️‍♂️

Lateral Raises – 3x12-15 ✈️

Face Pulls – 3x15 🔄

Hanging Leg Raises – 3x12-15 🦵

Russian Twists – 3x20/side 🔄

(Finish: 10 min rowing 🚣‍♂️)

Day 5️⃣ (Friday): Arms (Hypertrophy)

Close-Grip Bench – 3x10-12 🏋️‍♂️

EZ Bar Curls – 3x10-12 💪

Triceps Rope Pushdown – 3x12-15 🦾

Preacher Curls – 3x12 🔥

(Superset biceps/triceps for pump! 💥)

Day 6️⃣ (Saturday): Legs (Hamstrings/Glutes) + HIIT

Romanian Deadlifts – 4x8 🏋️‍♀️

Hip Thrusts – 3x10-12 🍑

Leg Curls – 3x12-15 🦵

Farmer's Walk – 3x30 sec 🚶‍♂️

HIIT Finisher:

30 sec Sprint / 30 sec Walk – 8 rounds ⚡

6-Day Calisthenics Plan

🏋️‍♂️ 6-Day Calisthenics Plan

Goal: Build strength 💪, muscle 🏋️‍♂️, and mobility 🧘‍♂️

Rest Day: Sunday (active recovery: yoga/stretching) 🧘‍♀️

🤸‍♂️ Skill-Based Moves (Advanced)

Muscle-Up (Pull-Up + Dip Combo) 🚪💥⬆️

Front Lever (Core & Back Strength) 🏋️‍♂️🌍

Planche (Shoulder & Core Mastery) �🔥

Human Flag (Full-Body Power) 🚩💪

🧘‍♂️ Cool Down

Static Stretching 🧘‍♂️

Deep Breathing 🌬️

Hydration 💧

💡 Pro Tips:

✔️ Consistency > Intensity – Progress gradually!

✔️ Form First – Avoid injuries.

✔️ Rest & Recovery – Muscles grow during rest! 💤

Detailed Workout Plan

Day 1️⃣ (Monday): Upper Body Push

Push-Ups – 4x12-15 🏋️‍♂️ (slow, full range)

Pike Push-Ups – 3x8-10 ✋ (shoulder focus)

Dips (Parallel Bars/Chair) – 3x8-10 🔄

Handstand Holds – 3x20-30 sec ⏳ (against wall)

Triceps Extensions – 3x10-12 🦾

(Finish: 5 min wrist mobility drills ✋)

Day 2️⃣ (Tuesday): Upper Body Pull

Pull-Ups – 4x6-8 🏋️‍♂️ (strict form)

Bodyweight Rows (Table/TRX) – 3x10-12 🔄

Archer Rows – 3x6/side 🎯 (scaled: feet on ground)

Hanging Leg Raises – 3x12-15 🦵

Plank to Failure – 3xMAX ⏳

(Stretch: 5 min lats & biceps 🧘‍♂️)

Day 3️⃣ (Wednesday): Legs & Core

Pistol Squats – 3x5/side 🦵 (assisted if needed)

Jump Squats – 3x12-15 ⚡

Nordic Hamstring Curls – 3x6-8 🏋️‍♀️ (slow negatives)

L-Sit Holds – 3x15-20 sec ⏳ (tucked if advanced)

Dragon Flags – 3x6-8 🐉 (scaled: negatives)

(Cool down: 5 min hip stretches 🧘‍♀️)

Day 4️⃣ (Thursday): Skill & Mobility

Handstand Practice – 10 min ✋ (wall holds/kicks)

Front Lever Progressions – 3x8-10 sec 🏋️‍♂️ (tuck/adv. tuck)

Planche Lean Holds – 3x20-30 sec ⏳

Skin the Cat – 3x5 🔄 (shoulder mobility)

Bridge Holds – 3x20 sec 🌉

(Focus: Form over reps! 👀)

Day 5️⃣ (Friday): Full Body HIIT

Burpees – 4x10 ⚡

Mountain Climbers – 3x20/side 🏔️

Explosive Push-Ups – 3x8 💥

Jump Lunges – 3x12/side 🦵

Plank Jacks – 3x30 sec ⏳

(Finisher: 5 min jump rope 🏃‍♂️)

Day 6️⃣ (Saturday): Freestyle & Play

Muscle-Up Progressions – 10 min 🏋️‍♂️

Human Flag Practice – 3x5 sec/side 🚩

Handstand Walks – 3x10 steps 🚶‍♂️

Parkour Basics – Vaults/Rolling 🏃‍♂️

(Have fun! 😄)
